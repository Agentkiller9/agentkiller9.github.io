## Welcome to My Blog!

This is the first post on my brand new blog, hosted directly on my portfolio website! I'm moving away from Medium to have more control over my content and presentation.

### What to Expect

Here, I'll be sharing my journey in the world of cybersecurity. You can expect to find:

-   **CTF Write-ups:** Detailed walkthroughs of challenges from platforms like Hack The Box and TryHackMe.
-   **Technical Deep Dives:** Exploring concepts in penetration testing, red teaming, and defensive security.
-   **Tool Reviews:** My thoughts on the tools I use daily.
-   **Career Advice:** Sharing what I learn as I progress in my career.

I'm excited to start this new chapter and share my knowledge with you. Stay tuned for more content!
